## 1. Za Njila

### Njila ndi chiyani?

Njila ndi nsanja yaulere ya upangiri wa ntchito pogwiritsa ntchito AI, yopangidwa kwa achinyamata aku Zambia, mothandizidwa ndi mgwirizano wa Ministry of Technical and Vocational Skills Development (MoTS) ya Zambia ndi World Bank Group. Kudzera mu ma chatbot olankhulana, Njila imathandiza ogwiritsa ntchito kuzindikira ndi kulemba maluso awo, kufufuza njira za ntchito zogwirizana ndi maluso amenewo, komanso kupeza mwayi wogwirizana ndi maluso ndi zokonda zawo.

### Ndani angagwiritse ntchito Njila?

Njila yapangidwa kwa ophunzira aku Zambia, omaliza maphunziro, ndi achinyamata omwe akufunafuna ntchito, kuphatikiza aliyense amene akufuna kumvetsa bwino maluso ake ndi zosankha za ntchito. Kugwiritsa ntchito ndi kwaulere ndipo sikufuna kuti munthu akhale ndi chidziwitso cha ntchito kale.

Ogwiritsa ntchito ayenera kukhala ndi zaka zosachepera 16 kuti agwiritse ntchito Njila. Ogwiritsa ntchito osakwanitsa zaka 18 angagwiritse ntchito Serviceyi pokhapokha atavomerezedwa ndi kholo kapena woyang'anira mwalamulo, ndipo pakhale kuyang'aniridwa.

### Ndingachite chiyani ndi Njila?

Njila ndi nsanja yomwe ingakuthandizeni kukonzekera msika wa ntchito.

- Mvetsetsani ndi kufotokoza bwino zomwe mwakumana nazo kusukulu, pa maphunziro, kuntchito, kunyumba, kapena mdera kukhala maluso omwe olemba ntchito amawayamikira.
- Pezani ma chat agent olumikizana omwe amakonzekeretsa ogwiritsa ntchito pa ntchito kudzera mu maphunziro othandiza monga kulemba CV ndi cover letter, kukonzekera malo antchito, ndi kukonzekera ma interview.
- Fufuzani njira za ntchito zomwe zilipo m'magawo osiyanasiyana ku Zambia, makamaka m'magawo ofunika ndi okukula monga ulimi, migodi, alendo ndi mahotela, mphamvu, ndi ena.
- Yang'anani ntchito zenizeni ndikulumikizidwa ndi ntchito zomwe zikugwirizana ndi maluso, zokonda, ndi maphunziro a wogwiritsa ntchito, kudzera pa nsanjayi.

### Kodi ndiyenera kulipira kuti ndigwiritse ntchito Njila?

Ayi. Njila ndi yaulere kwathunthu. Palibe ndalama zolipira potsegula akaunti, kucheza ndi AI coach, kapena kutsitsa lipoti la maluso.

## 2. Kuyambira Ntchito: Kulembetsa & Kulowa

### Ikundiuza kuti akaunti yanga sinatsimikizidwe ndikayesera kulowa.

Pamene akaunti yalembetsedwa ndi imelo/password, Njila imafuna kutsimikizira imelo. Tsegulani imelo yochokera ku "Njila", kenako dinani ulalo wotsimikizira kuti akauntiyo iyambe kugwira ntchito.

### Sindinalandire imelo yotsimikizira.

Nthawi zina maimelo otsimikizira amalowa m'mafoda ena:

- Yang'anani foda ya spam/junk ya imelo yomwe munagwiritsa ntchito.
- Onetsetsani kuti mukuyang'ana adiresi ya imelo yomwe munalemba pa kulembetsa.
- Dikirani mphindi zochepa ndiyeno yang'ananinso - maimelo angachedwe kufika.

### Ndayesa kulembetsa, koma ikuti imelo yanga ikugwiritsidwa kale ntchito.

Izi zikutanthauza kuti akaunti yokhala ndi imeloyo ilipo kale. Ngati simukumbukira password:

- Pitani ku [https://njila.ai/](https://njila.ai/)
- Lowetsani imelo yomwe munali kugwiritsa ntchito potsegula akaunti.
- Dinani "Forgot Password" pansi pa malo a Password.
- Mutha kuyang'ananso ngati mudalowapo ku Njila pogwiritsa ntchito Google. Pa tsamba lolowera, dinani "Login with Google" ndikusankha imeloyo.

### Ndayiwala password yanga.

Kuti mukonzenso password yanu:

1. Pitani ku [https://njila.ai/](https://njila.ai/)
2. Dinani ulalo wa "Forgot Password".
3. Lowetsani adiresi ya imelo yolembetsedwa.
4. Dinani "Send Reset Email".
5. Yang'anani inbox ya imelo kuti mupeze ulalo wokonzanso password.
6. Dinani ulalowo ndikutatira malangizo kuti mupange password yatsopano.

### Sindinalandire imelo yokonzanso password.

Ngati simunalandire imelo yokonzanso, yesani izi:

- Yang'anani mafoda a spam, junk, kapena promotions.
- Onetsetsani kuti adiresi ya imelo yomwe yalowetsedwa ndi yolondola.
- Dikirani mphindi zochepa ndikusintha inbox yanu - kutumiza imelo kungachedwe.
- Tumizaninso reset ya password patatha masekondi 60.

### Ulalo wotsimikizira imelo sukugwira ndikadina.

- **Koperani ndi kuyika:** m'malo modina, koperani URL yonse ndikuyiyika mu address bar ya browser.
- **Yang'anani kusweka kwa mizere:** mapulogalamu a imelo nthawi zina amaswa ma URL ataliatali m'mizere ingapo - onetsetsani kuti URL yonse yakopedwa.
- **Pemphani ulalo watsopano:** ulalo ukhoza kukhala wataya nthawi; lembetsaninso kapena pemphani imelo yatsopano yotsimikizira.

### Akaunti yanga yatsekedwa nditalowetsa password yolakwika kangapo.

Chifukwa cha chitetezo, Njila imatsekereza kwakanthawi kuyesa kulowa kuchokera pa chipangizo pamene password yalowetsedwa molakwika mobwerezabwereza. Kuti mukonze izi:

- Dikirani kanthawi kochepa musanayesenso.
- Gwiritsani ntchito ulalo wa "Forgot Password" pa tsamba lolowera kuti mukonzenso password - iyi ndi njira yachangu yobwereranso.
- Ngati kutsekedwaku kukupitirira, kapena akaunti ikuoneka ngati yaletsedwa, tumizani imelo ku [hi@njila.ai](mailto:hi@njila.ai) kuti mupeze thandizo.

## 3. Chatbot Sikuyankha

### Chatbot ikuwonetsa loading koma siyankha.

Izi nthawi zambiri zikutanthauza vuto la netiweki kapena server. Yesani:

- **Yang'anani intaneti:** onetsetsani kuti kulumikizana kuli kolimba.
- **Sinthani tsambalo (refresh):** dinani F5 kapena Ctrl+R (Cmd+R pa Mac).
- **Dikirani masekondi 30-120:** AI ikhoza kukhala ikukonza pempho lovuta.
- **Yang'anani banner ya offline:** chidziwitso cha offline pamwamba chikutanthauza kuti intaneti yalekana.

### Ndikalemba uthenga ndikanikiza send, palibe chimachitika.

Yang'anani zifukwa izi zomwe zimachitika kawirikawiri:

- **Offline:** yang'anani banner ya offline pamwamba pa tsamba.
- **Kutalika kwa uthenga:** mauthenga sayenera kupitirira zilembo 1000.
- **Batani latha ntchito:** batani la send limazimitsidwa mukakhala offline, palibe mawu, kapena mawu apitirira zilembo 1000.

### Uthenga wanga watumizidwa koma chatbot siyayankha.

Ngati uthengawo ukuoneka koma palibe yankho:

- Dikirani masekondi 30-120 - kukonza kwa AI kumatenga nthawi.
- Yang'anani ngati pali mauthenga a zolakwa mu chat.
- Sinthani tsambalo kuti muwone ngati yankho likuwoneka.
- Yang'anani momwe netiweki yalumikizidwira.

### Ndikuona cholakwa "Something went wrong" kapena "Cannot connect to the service".

Zolakwa izi nthawi zambiri zimatantauza:

- **Vuto la netiweki:** "Cannot connect to the service" likusonyeza vuto la kulumikizana ndi intaneti.
- **Vuto la server:** backend service ikhoza kukhala ndi mavuto.
- **Rate limiting:** mapempho ochuluka kwambiri munthawi yochepa.
- **Zolowetsa zosaloledwa:** uthenga ukhoza kukhala ndi zilembo kapena kapangidwe kosathandizidwa.

Kuti mukonze:

- Yang'anani intaneti.
- Dikirani mphindi 1-2 ndiyeno yesaninso.
- Sinthani tsambalo.
- Ngati cholakwikacho chikupitirira, service ikhoza kukhala yatsika kwakanthawi.

### Chatbot ikutenga nthawi yayitali kuyankha (masekondi 30+).

Mayankho ochedwa angayambitsidwe ndi zinthu izi kumbali ya wogwiritsa ntchito:

- Chizindikiro chofooka cha foni - sinthani kupita ku Wi-Fi ngati mukugwiritsa ntchito data ya pa foni.
- Intaneti yochedwa - yang'anani liwiro la kulumikizana.
- Kuchulukana kwa netiweki pa masukulu kapena ma network a makampani pa nthawi yotanganidwa.
- Ma tab ambiri mu browser - tsekani osafunika kuti chipangizo chisagwiritse ntchito mphamvu zambiri.

Ngati mayankho nthawi zonse akutenga kupitirira masekondi 120, pakhoza kukhala vuto la server.

### Ndikuona chizindikiro cha typing koma mauthenga anga akale sakuwoneka.

Ngati mbiri ya zokambirana ikulephera kutseguka:

- Sinthani tsambalo (F5).
- Yang'anani kulumikizana kwa intaneti.
- Chotsani cache ya browser ndikulowetsanso tsambalo.
- Tulukani mu akaunti ndiyeno lowaninso.

Kwa ogwiritsa ntchito olembetsedwa, mbiri ya zokambirana imasungidwa nthawi zonse ndipo iyenera kubweranso yokha.

### Kodi ndingagwiritse ntchito Njila pa foni kapena tablet?

Inde. Njila imasinthika bwino pa kukula kwa chinsalu ndipo imagwira ntchito pa:

- Mafoni a m'manja (iOS, Android).
- Ma tablet (iPad, ma tablet a Android).
- Makompyuta a desktop.

Mawonekedwe amasinthika mogwirizana ndi kukula kwa chinsalu.

## 4. Mavuto a Zokambirana & Session

### Kodi ndingathe kupeza zokambirana zanga kuchokera pa chipangizo china?

Inde, kwa maakaunti olembetsedwa (imelo/password kapena Google Sign-In):

- Lowani pa chipangizo chatsopano pogwiritsa ntchito zidziwitso zomwezi.
- Mbiri ya zokambirana idzatseguka yokha.
- Ma session amalumikizidwa pa zipangizo zonse.

### Chatbot ikutumiza uthenga womwewo kangapo.

Mayankho obwerezabwereza angachitike ngati:

- **Send yadinidwa kangapo:** kudina kulikonse kumatumiza pempho latsopano.
- **Browser yasinthidwa nthawi yokonza:** kusintha tsamba pomwe mukudikira yankho.
- **Vuto la kumbuyo kwa server:** backend ikhoza kukhala yakonza uthenga womwewo kawiri.

Kuti mupewe izi:

- Dinani batani la send kamodzi kokha.
- Dikirani yankho musanatumize uthenga wina.
- Musasinthe tsamba mukamadikira yankho.

Ngati kubwerezabwereza kukupitirira, lembani momwe zinachitikira ndikutumiza lipoti la vutolo.

### Chatbot ikudzitsutsa yokha kapena kuiwala zomwe tanena kale mu zokambirana.

Izi zingachitike pa zokambirana zazitali kwambiri chifukwa cha:

- **Kufupikitsa zokambirana:** zambiri zakale zimafupikitsidwa.
- **Malire a context window:** AI ingaganizire gawo lina lokha la mbiri.
- **Kusintha phase:** kusamukira ku phase yatsopano kungasinthe zomwe zikuyang'aniridwa.

Ngati zambiri zofunika zaiwalika, zikumbutseni chatbot mu uthenga waposachedwa. Zokambirana zaposachedwa zimakhudza kwambiri mayankho.

## 5. Netiweki & Kulumikizana

### Njila ikuchedwa kwambiri pa intaneti yanga. Kodi ndingagwiritsabe ntchito?

Njila yapangidwa kuti igwire ntchito ngakhale pa kulumikizana kochepa, koma kuthamanga kwake kungakhudzidwe.

Malangizo ogwiritsa ntchito Njila pa intaneti yochedwa:

- Tsekani ma tab kapena mapulogalamu ena omwe akugwiritsa ntchito bandwidth.
- Zimitsani kuwonera makanema m'mawindo ena.
- Gwiritsani ntchito Wi-Fi m'malo mwa data ya pa foni ngati ilipo.
- Pewani nthawi yotanganidwa pa ma network ogawana.
- Khalani oleza mtima - dikirani tsamba litseguke lonse musanayambe kugwiritsa ntchito.

### Kodi ndingagwiritse ntchito Njila popanda intaneti (offline)?

Ayi, Njila imafuna kulumikizana kwa intaneti komwe kuli kugwira ntchito. Kukonza konse kwa zokambirana kumachitika pa server, osati pa chipangizo chanu.

### Nchiyani chimachitika ngati intaneti yanga yatheka ndikamagwiritsa ntchito Njila?

Mukakhala offline:

- Banner ya chidziwitso imawoneka pamwamba pa chinsalu kusonyeza kuti muli offline.
- Mabatani onse ndi malo olowetsa zimazimitsidwa - mauthenga sangatumizidwe ndipo simungayende m'magawo.
- Zokambirana zomwe zilipo zikuwonekabe - mbiri ya mauthenga ikhoza kuwerengedwa.
- Palibe data imatayika - zonse zimapitiriza mukalumikizananso.

### Ndikuona banner ya "offline", koma ndalumikizidwa ku Wi-Fi.

Izi nthawi zambiri zimatanthauza kuti Wi-Fi yalumikizidwa koma intaneti yeniyeni sikufika pa chipangizo. Yesani izi:

- Tsegulani webusaiti ina (mwachitsanzo google.com) mu tab yatsopano - ngati siyikutsegukanso, vuto lili pa intaneti yanu osati pa Njila.
- Yang'anani ngati zipangizo zina pa Wi-Fi yomweyo zikutha kufika pa intaneti.
- Yambitsaninso router kapena modem ya Wi-Fi.
- Sinthani kupita ku data ya pa foni kuti muwone ngati vuto lili pa netiweki ya Wi-Fi.
- Ma Wi-Fi ena apagulu (m'ma cafe, mahotela, ma campus) amafuna kulowa pa tsamba lolandirira kaye kuti intaneti igwire ntchito.

## 6. Mavuto a Data & Kutulutsa Mafayilo

### Ndikufuna kutsitsa lipoti langa la maluso koma sindikupeza njira yake.

Kuti mutsitse lipoti la maluso:

- Fufuzani gawo la "Experiences" kapena "Skills" mu navigation.
- Pezani batani la "Download Report" kapena export.
- Sankhani format - PDF kapena DOCX (chikalata cha Word).
- Lipotilo liyenera kutsika lokha mu foda ya downloads yomwe imagwiritsidwa ntchito nthawi zonse.

Ngati njira yotsitsa sikuwoneka:

- Mungafunike kupitiriza patsogolo mu zokambirana (kumaliza phase ya skills discovery).
- Mbaliyi ikhoza kukhala isatsegulidwe pa mtundu wa akaunti yomwe mukugwiritsa ntchito.
- Onetsetsani kuti chipangizo sichili offline - mabatani otsitsa amazimitsidwa mukakhala offline.

### Ndikanikiza download koma ndimapeza cholakwa m'malo mwa fayilo.

Zolakwa popanga lipoti zingayambitsidwe ndi:

- **Kusowa kwa data:** zokambirana zikhoza kukhala zosakwanira kupanga lipoti.
- **Kugwirizana kwa browser:** ma browser akale kwambiri sangathandize kupanga PDF.
- **Mavuto a memory:** malipoti akulu kwambiri angalephere pa zipangizo zomwe zili ndi memory yochepa.

Mayankho:

- Onetsetsani kuti phase ya skills discovery yatha.
- Sinthani browser yanu kukhala version yatsopano.
- Tsekani ma tab ena kuti memory ikhale yaulere.
- Yesani format ya DOCX ngati PDF ikulephera.
- Yesani chipangizo china kapena browser ina.

### PDF ilibe kanthu kapena ikusowa zambiri.

Ngati lipoti latsika koma zambiri zikusowa:

- Yang'anani kuti zambiri zonse zofunika zinaperekedwa mu zokambirana.
- Yendetsani PDF yonse - zina zingakhale pa masamba otsatira.
- Yesani format ya DOCX kuti muwone ngati zambiri zikuwoneka kumeneko.
- Tsitsaninso - nthawi zina kupanga kumakhala ndi zolakwika zazing'ono.
- Pitirizani zokambirana ndiyeno yesaninso kutsitsa mutawonjezera zambiri.

### Lipoti langa la maluso likuwonetsa zambiri zosiyana ndi zomwe tinakambirana.

Lipotilo limakhala ndi zambiri zomwe zinatsimikiziridwa pa zokambirana. Kusiyana kungachitike ngati:

- **Maluso anawonjezedwa kenako anachotsedwa:** maluso otsimikizika omaliza okha ndi omwe amaoneka.
- **Kusintha kangapo:** version yatsopano kwambiri ya data ndi yomwe imagwiritsidwa ntchito.
- **Ma session osiyana a zokambirana:** onetsetsani kuti session yolondola yasankhidwa.
- **Data sinatsimikizidwe:** zambiri zikhoza kukambidwa koma osazitsimikiza komaliza.

Ngati zambiri zina zikuoneka kuti ndi zolakwika:

- Unikani zokambirana kuti muwone zomwe zinatsimikiziridwa zenizeni.
- Pitirizani zokambirana ndikusintha kapena kukonza zolakwika.
- Tsitsaninso lipotilo mutasintha.

### Zina mwa zomwe ndakumana nazo kapena maluso anga zikusowa mu lipoti.

Yang'anani ngati:

- Phase yoyenera ya zokambirana inamalizidwa - zomwe zatsirizidwa zokha ndi zomwe zimaphatikizidwa.
- Zinthu zina zinachotsedwa pa zokambirana.
- Session yolakwika ndiyo ikugwira ntchito.
- Phase ina sinamalizidwe kwathunthu - ma phase ena ayenera kumalizidwa kuti data itulutsidwe.

Unikani mbiri ya zokambirana kuti mutsimikize zomwe zinavomerezedwa ndi kutsirizidwa.

## 9. Zachinsinsi & Migwirizano

### Ndi data yanji yomwe Njila imasonkhanitsa za ine?

Njila imasonkhanitsa zambiri zokha zofunika kuti service igwire ntchito:

- **Zambiri za akaunti:** dzina ndi adiresi ya imelo.
- **Zambiri zoyambira za anthu:** gulu la zaka, chinenero chomwe mumakonda, ndi malo ambiri omwe muli.
- **Usage analytics:** momwe mumagwiritsira ntchito nsanja, kuphatikiza zambiri zaukadaulo monga mtundu wa chipangizo, browser, ndi operating system.
- **Zokambirana:** mauthenga anu ndi mayankho a Njila.
- **Zotsatira za maluso:** zotsatira za kuyesa maluso ndi malingaliro a ntchito.

Njila sisonkhanitsa data yachinsinsi kwambiri monga za umoyo, zizindikiro za biometric, kapena zambiri za mtundu wa anthu kapena fuko. Kuti muwone mndandanda wonse wa data yomwe imasonkhanitsidwa ndi momwe imagwiritsidwira ntchito, onani [Privacy Policy](https://njila.ai/#/privacy-policy).

### Kodi data ya zokambirana zanga ndi yachinsinsi komanso yotetezeka?

Njila imayendetsedwa ndi Tabiya, pulojekiti yomwe imathandizidwa pa zachuma ndi Global Development Incubator (GDI). Data ya zokambirana ndi zambiri zaumwini zimabisidwa (encrypted) potumiza ndi posunga, ndipo zimasungidwa pa ma server a Google Cloud. Zambiri zina zofunika (monga dzina ndi zaka) zimabisidwa pa chipangizo chanu zisanatumizidwe. Inu nokha ndi amene mutha kuona mbiri ya zokambirana zanu; oyang'anira system ovomerezeka angapeze data pa ntchito za support ndi maintenance.

Njila imagwiritsa ntchito zida za analytics kuti imvetse momwe nsanjayi imagwiritsidwira ntchito ndi kupititsa patsogolo zomwe ogwiritsa ntchito amakumana nazo. Kuti muwone zonse za data yomwe imasonkhanitsidwa, momwe imagwiritsidwira ntchito, ndi ufulu wanu, onani [Privacy Policy](https://njila.ai/#/privacy-policy).

### Ndani angawone mbiri ya zokambirana zanga?

Kupezeka kwa zokambirana zanu:

- **Inu:** muli ndi mwayi wonse woona zokambirana zanu zonse.
- **Oyang'anira system:** angakhale ndi mwayi wopeza data pa ntchito za support kapena maintenance.
- **Bungwe lanu (ngati likugwira):** lingakhale ndi mwayi pa data yophatikizidwa kapena yosadziwika dzina, kutengera mgwirizano.
- **Ogwiritsa ntchito ena:** sangathe kuona zokambirana zanu.

Onani privacy policy kuti mumvetse mfundo zenizeni za kupezeka ndi kusunga data.
