# Hospitality & Tourism Sector Profile - Zambia

## Key Facts

- **Employment:** 102,648 (LFS 2023, total employment incl. informal); 42,797 (EEI 2023, Accommodation & Food formal sector)
- **Average monthly earnings:** K3,955 (EEI 2023, Accommodation & Food Service formal sector average)
- **Critical occupations identified:** 14
- **TEVET accredited programmes:** 33
- **Training institutions:** 155
- **Programmes in development:** 0
