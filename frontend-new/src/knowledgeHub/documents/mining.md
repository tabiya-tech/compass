# Mining Sector Profile - Zambia

## Key Facts

- **Employment:** 90,075 (LFS 2023, total employment including informal); 58,077 (EEI 2023, formal sector)
- **Average monthly earnings:** K14,182 (EEI 2023, formal sector average — highest of all industrial sectors)
- **Critical occupations identified:** 34
- **TEVET accredited programmes:** 103
- **Training institutions:** 124
- **Programmes in development:** 37
