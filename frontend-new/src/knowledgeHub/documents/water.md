# Water & Sanitation Sector Profile - Zambia

## Key Facts

- **Employment:** 16,589 (LFS 2023, total employment); 11,314 (EEI 2023, Water Supply, Sewerage & Waste Management formal sector)
- **Average monthly earnings:** K7,813 (EEI 2023, Water Supply, Sewerage & Waste Management formal sector average)
- **Critical occupations identified:** 82
- **TEVET accredited programmes:** 25
- **Training institutions:** 58
- **Programmes in development:** 0

## TEVET Accredited Programmes

25 accredited programmes across 58 institutions.
