# Energy Sector Profile - Zambia

## Key Facts

- **Employment:** 6,539 (LFS 2023, total employment); 5,534 (EEI 2023, formal sector)
- **Average monthly earnings:** K13,607 (EEI 2023, formal sector average — second-highest of all industrial sectors)
- **Critical occupations identified:** 32
- **TEVET accredited programmes:** 55
- **Training institutions:** 126
- **Programmes in development:** 23
