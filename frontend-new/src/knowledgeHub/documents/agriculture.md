# Agriculture Sector Profile - Zambia

## Key Facts

- **Employment:** 1,003,847 (LFS 2023, total employment incl. informal — largest sector); 70,763 (EEI 2023, formal sector)
- **Average monthly earnings:** K3,746 (EEI 2023, formal sector average)
- **Critical occupations identified:** 47
- **TEVET accredited programmes:** 56
- **Training institutions:** 234
- **Programmes in development:** 21
